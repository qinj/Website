Hi! Welcome to The BAR !

We are a lifegroup within the community of Harvest Mission Community Church. The BAR is an acronym for The Broken And Redeemed. We believe that our life group should represent a casual, yet open environment akin to a bar. Various people go to a bar. Some might be celebration an achievement, others might be going to drink off a harsh setback. We advocate people to come as they are no matter what season of life they are in.

But even if I am being poured out like a drink offering on the sacrifice and service coming from your faith, I am glad and rejoice with all of you. So you too should be glad and rejoice with me. Phillipians 2.17-18
